The Webchat allows you to integrate your bot into your website. It can be used in a variety of ways, from a simple injection script to a fully cusomizable React library.

<Tip>
  If you are looking for a way to integrate your bot in your backend, check out the [Chat API](/api-reference/chat-api/introduction).
</Tip>
