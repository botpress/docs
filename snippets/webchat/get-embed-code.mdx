To embed a bot on your website, you need the bot's Webchat embed code. You can get the embed code from the [Dashboard](/learn/get-started/dashboard/):

1. Open your bot's Workspace and select the bot you want to embed.
2. In the left sidebar, go to **Webchat**, then open the Share tab.
3. Copy the **Embed code**:
